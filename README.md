Customer Churn Analysis — EDA
📌 Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis on a Telecom Customer Churn dataset to identify key drivers behind customer attrition and provide actionable business recommendations.
🛠️ Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
📊 Dataset
7,043 customer records with 21 features including contract type, payment method, internet service, and demographics.
🔍 Key Findings
Month-to-month customers churn significantly more than long-term contract holders. Electronic check users have the highest churn rate due to transaction friction. Fiber optic customers churn despite paying premium prices suggesting a price vs value mismatch. Senior citizens show higher churn rates than non-seniors. Customers without TechSupport or OnlineSecurity are more likely to churn.
💡 Business Recommendations
Incentivize month-to-month customers to switch to annual contracts. Offer autopay discounts to move electronic check users to automatic payments. Investigate fiber optic pricing and service quality. Bundle TechSupport and OnlineSecurity into core plans. Create targeted retention campaigns for senior citizens.
